Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Kolpa AI is an AI-powered entertainment application. To provide our core functionality, the app requires users to upload chat screenshots or text.

  • Zero Retention Policy: We do not store, save, or log your screenshots, texts, or analysis results on our servers. All data is processed in real-time and immediately discarded after the AI generates a response.
  • No Personal Data Harvesting: We do not collect names, phone numbers, or metadata from the uploaded screenshots.

2. Third-Party AI Processing

To analyze the provided text and generate advice, Kolpa AI securely transmits the anonymized text data to third-party AI service providers (e.g., Google Gemini API). These providers process the data strictly to generate the response and are prohibited from using your data to train their models in a way that identifies you.

3. Contact Us

For any privacy-related requests, contact us at: support@kolpaai.me
